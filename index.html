About

LR4Health focuses on longitudinal reasoning systems capable of integrating evolving multimodal clinical evidence across patient trajectories. The workshop brings together researchers working on multimodal reasoning, retrieval-grounded inference, uncertainty-aware clinical AI, deployment challenges, clinician supervision, and trustworthy healthcare AI systems.

The workshop emphasizes challenges involving temporal reasoning, calibration under distribution shift, evidence attribution, longitudinal decision support, and deployment of multimodal foundation models in real-world healthcare environments.
